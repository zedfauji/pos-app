# Video Script: Processing a Payment

**Video Title:** "How to Process a Payment"  
**Duration:** 5 minutes  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Target Audience:** Servers

---

## Script

### [INTRODUCTION - 15 seconds]

"Welcome back to MagiDesk POS training. In this video, we'll learn how to process a payment when customers are ready to pay. This is the final step in serving customers. Let's get started."

**[PAUSE - Show occupied table with order]**

---

### [MAIN CONTENT - 4 minutes 20 seconds]

#### Step 1: Stopping the Table Session (25 seconds)

"When customers are ready to pay, the first thing you need to do is stop the table session. Click on the occupied table - you'll see it's red."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Click on red table]**

"Then click the 'Stop Session' button."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Click "Stop Session" button]**

"The system will generate a bill showing all the items, the time cost if applicable, and the total amount."

---

#### Step 2: Reviewing the Bill (30 seconds)

"Before processing payment, review the bill carefully."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Show bill details]**

"You'll see: the list of all items ordered, the quantity and price for each item, the time cost if the table charges by time, any discounts if applied, tax if applicable, and the total amount due."

**[HIGHLIGHT: Each section of the bill]**

"Make sure everything looks correct. If something is wrong, you can go back and fix it before processing payment."

---

#### Step 3: Opening the Payment Screen (15 seconds)

"Once you've reviewed the bill and everything looks good, click the 'Process Payment' button."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Click "Process Payment" button]**

"The payment screen will appear."

---

#### Step 4: Understanding Payment Methods (30 seconds)

"On the payment screen, you'll see different payment methods available."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Show payment methods]**

"Common payment methods include: Cash, Credit Card, Debit Card, Mobile Payment like Apple Pay or Google Pay, and sometimes Wallet or Store Credit."

**[HIGHLIGHT: Each payment method]**

"Select the payment method the customer wants to use."

---

#### Step 5: Entering Payment Amount (25 seconds)

"For cash payments, you'll need to enter the amount the customer is giving you."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Enter cash amount]**

"For card or mobile payments, the amount is usually pre-filled with the bill total, but you can adjust it if needed."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Show pre-filled amount]**

"Make sure the payment amount equals the bill total, unless the customer is paying with a specific amount and needs change."

---

#### Step 6: Processing Cash Payment (30 seconds)

"If the customer is paying with cash, enter the amount they're giving you."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Enter cash amount, e.g., $50 for a $45 bill]**

"The system will calculate the change automatically."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Show change calculation]**

"Review the change amount, then click 'Process Payment'."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Click "Process Payment"]**

"Count out the change and give it to the customer along with the receipt."

---

#### Step 7: Processing Card Payment (30 seconds)

"If the customer is paying with a card, select 'Credit Card' or 'Debit Card'."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Select card payment]**

"The amount should already be filled in. Click 'Process Payment'."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Click "Process Payment"]**

"If you have a card reader, the customer will insert or tap their card. Follow the prompts on the card reader."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Show card reader if applicable]**

"Once the payment is approved, you'll see a confirmation."

---

#### Step 8: Processing Mobile Payment (25 seconds)

"If the customer is paying with a mobile payment like Apple Pay or Google Pay, select that option."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Select mobile payment]**

"Click 'Process Payment', and the customer will use their phone to complete the payment."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Show mobile payment process if applicable]**

"Once approved, you'll see a confirmation."

---

#### Step 9: Payment Confirmation (20 seconds)

"After the payment is processed successfully, you'll see a confirmation message."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Show confirmation]**

"The table will turn green again, showing it's available for new customers."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Show table turning green]**

"The payment is complete, and the receipt will print automatically."

---

#### Step 10: Printing Receipts (20 seconds)

"After payment, the receipt will print automatically to your default printer."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Show receipt printing]**

"Give the receipt to the customer. If the receipt didn't print, you can click 'Print Receipt' to print it manually."

**[DEMONSTRATE: Show "Print Receipt" button if needed]**

---

#### Step 11: What If Payment Fails? (20 seconds)

"If a payment fails, you'll see an error message."

**[SHOW: Error message example if applicable]**

"Common reasons include: insufficient funds, card declined, or network error."

"For card payments, ask the customer to try again or use a different card. For other issues, contact your manager."

---

### [SUMMARY - 15 seconds]

"To summarize: Stop the table session, review the bill, click 'Process Payment', select the payment method, enter the amount if needed, click 'Process Payment', wait for confirmation, and give the customer their receipt."

---

### [OUTRO - 10 seconds]

"Thanks for watching. In the next video, we'll learn how to handle split payments when customers want to pay separately. See you then!"

---

## Visual Cues

### What to Show on Screen

1. **Occupied table (red)** - Show clearly
2. **"Stop Session" button** - Highlight when clicking
3. **Bill details** - Show all sections clearly
4. **"Process Payment" button** - Highlight when clicking
5. **Payment methods** - Show all options
6. **Cash amount entry** - Show entering amount
7. **Change calculation** - Show change amount
8. **Card payment** - Show selection and process
9. **Mobile payment** - Show if applicable
10. **Confirmation message** - Show success
11. **Table turning green** - Show the change
12. **Receipt printing** - Show if visible
13. **Error message** - Show example if applicable

### Annotations to Add (Optional)

- **Arrow pointing to "Stop Session" button**
- **Highlight bill sections** (items, time, total)
- **Arrow pointing to "Process Payment" button**
- **Highlight payment methods**
- **Text label: "Change"** on change amount
- **Checkmark on confirmation**
- **Green circle on table**

## Recording Notes

### Before Recording

- [ ] Have test table with order ready
- [ ] Know payment methods available
- [ ] Test card reader if applicable
- [ ] Close all other applications
- [ ] Turn off notifications
- [ ] Test microphone

### During Recording

- **Speak slowly and clearly**
- **Pause 2-3 seconds between steps**
- **Wait for screen to load before continuing**
- **If you make a mistake, pause and say "Let me correct that"**

### After Recording

- [ ] Watch the video
- [ ] Check audio is clear
- [ ] Verify all steps are visible
- [ ] Add annotations if needed
- [ ] Export in 1080p MP4 format

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Don't:**
- Rush through payment steps
- Skip reviewing the bill
- Forget to explain change calculation
- Assume viewers know payment methods
- Record at low resolution

✅ **Do:**
- Pause between steps
- Show bill review clearly
- Explain change calculation
- Use simple language
- Record in 1080p
