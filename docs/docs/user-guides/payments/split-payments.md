# Split Payments

This guide explains how to process split payments when customers want to pay using multiple payment methods.

---

## What is a Split Payment?

**In Simple Terms:** A split payment is when one bill is paid using multiple payment methods.

**Example:**
- Bill total: $100.00
- Customer 1 pays: $50.00 with card
- Customer 2 pays: $30.00 with cash
- Customer 3 pays: $20.00 with mobile payment
- Total: $100.00 ✅

---

## When to Use Split Payments

**Use split payments when:**
- Multiple people want to pay separately
- Customer wants to pay part card, part cash
- Group wants to split the bill
- Customer wants to use multiple payment methods

**Common scenarios:**
- Two friends splitting 50/50
- Three people splitting equally
- One person pays card, other pays cash
- Custom amounts per person

---

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Stop the Table Session

**First, generate the bill:**

1. Click on occupied table
2. Click "Stop Session"
3. Review the bill total
4. Note the total amount

**Example:** Bill total is $100.00

### Step 2: Open Payment Screen

**Click "Process Payment"**

**Payment screen appears**

### Step 3: Select Split Payment

**Click "Split Payment" button or option**

**Split payment screen appears:**
- Shows bill total
- Shows payment method options
- Shows amount inputs for each method
- Shows remaining balance

### Step 4: Enter Payment Amounts

**Enter amount for each payment method:**

**Example - Three people splitting $100:**

**Method 1: Card**
- Select "Card"
- Enter amount: $50.00

**Method 2: Cash**
- Select "Cash"
- Enter amount: $30.00

**Method 3: Mobile Payment**
- Select "Mobile Payment"
- Enter amount: $20.00

**System shows:**
- Amount entered: $100.00
- Remaining: $0.00
- Status: ✅ Balanced

### Step 5: Verify Total

**Check that split amounts add up:**

**Important:** Split total MUST equal bill total

**If balanced:**
- ✅ Shows "Balanced" or green checkmark
- ✅ Remaining: $0.00
- ✅ Can process payment

**If not balanced:**
- ❌ Shows "Not Balanced" or red warning
- ❌ Shows remaining amount
- ❌ Cannot process until balanced

**To fix:**
- Adjust amounts
- Use "Auto-Fill Remaining" if available
- Ensure total equals bill

### Step 6: Add Tips (Optional)

**If customers want to add tips:**

**Option 1: Split Tip Equally**
- Enter total tip amount
- System splits tip across all payments

**Option 2: Tip on One Method**
- Add tip to specific payment method
- Example: $5 tip on card payment only

**Option 3: Custom Tip Distribution**
- Enter tip amount for each method
- Example: $3 on card, $2 on cash

### Step 7: Apply Discounts (If Applicable)

**If discount is approved:**

**Discount can be:**
- Applied proportionally (split across all methods)
- Applied to one method only
- Split equally across methods

**Example:**
- $10 discount
- Applied proportionally:
  - Card: $5 discount
  - Cash: $3 discount
  - Mobile: $2 discount

### Step 8: Review Split Payment Summary

**Before processing, review:**

**Payment Breakdown:**
- Card: $50.00
- Cash: $30.00
- Mobile: $20.00
- **Total: $100.00** ✅

**Tips (if any):**
- Card tip: $X.XX
- Cash tip: $X.XX
- Mobile tip: $X.XX
- **Total tip: $X.XX**

**Final Totals:**
- Bill total: $100.00
- Discount: $X.XX (if any)
- Tip: $X.XX (if any)
- **Total to pay: $XXX.XX**

### Step 9: Process Split Payment

**Click "Process Split Payment"**

**What happens:**
- ✅ All payments are processed
- ✅ Each payment method is recorded separately
- ✅ Payment is recorded in system
- ✅ Bill status changes to "Paid"
- ✅ Receipt is generated
- ✅ Receipt shows breakdown of all payments

**Receipt shows:**
- Payment 1: Card - $50.00
- Payment 2: Cash - $30.00
- Payment 3: Mobile - $20.00
- Total: $100.00

## Split Payment Methods

### Equal Split

**When customers want to split equally:**

**Example - 3 people, $90 bill:**
- Each person pays: $30.00
- Method 1: $30.00
- Method 2: $30.00
- Method 3: $30.00
- Total: $90.00 ✅

**How to do it:**
- Divide bill by number of people
- Enter same amount for each method
- Or use "Equal Split" button if available

### Percentage Split

**When customers want to split by percentage:**

**Example - 2 people, $100 bill:**
- Person 1 pays 60%: $60.00
- Person 2 pays 40%: $40.00
- Total: $100.00 ✅

**How to do it:**
- Enter percentages
- System calculates amounts
- Or calculate manually and enter amounts

### Custom Amount Split

**When customers want to pay different amounts:**

**Example - 4 people, $100 bill:**
- Person 1: $40.00
- Person 2: $30.00
- Person 3: $20.00
- Person 4: $10.00
- Total: $100.00 ✅

**How to do it:**
- Enter custom amount for each
- Verify total equals bill
- Process payment

### Auto-Fill Remaining

**If available, this feature:**
- Automatically fills remaining amount
- Ensures total equals bill
- Prevents errors

**How to use:**
- Enter amounts for some methods
- Click "Auto-Fill Remaining"
- Last method gets remaining amount
- Verify and process

## Important Notes

### ⚠️ Validation Rules

**The system requires:**
- ✅ Split total MUST equal bill total
- ✅ Each amount must be > 0
- ✅ At least 2 payment methods
- ✅ All required fields filled

**If validation fails:**
- Error message appears
- Cannot process payment
- Fix amounts and try again

### ✅ Best Practices

- Verify split total equals bill total
- Review breakdown with customers
- Use "Auto-Fill Remaining" if available
- Double-check amounts before processing
- Print receipt showing breakdown

### ❌ Common Mistakes

- Split total doesn't equal bill (most common!)
- Entering wrong amounts
- Forgetting to include all payments
- Not verifying before processing

---

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Split Total Doesn't Match Bill"

**What it means:** Amounts don't add up to bill total

**Example:**
- Bill: $100.00
- Split: $50 + $30 + $15 = $95.00 ❌
- Missing: $5.00

**Solution:**
- Check all amounts
- Ensure total equals bill
- Adjust amounts
- Use "Auto-Fill Remaining" if available

### Problem: "Cannot Process Split Payment"

**Possible causes:**
- Amounts don't add up
- Missing payment method
- System error

**Solutions:**
1. Verify all amounts
2. Check total equals bill
3. Try again
4. Contact manager if issue persists

### Problem: "One Payment Method Failed"

**What to do:**
- Other payments may have processed
- Check which ones succeeded
- Process failed payment separately
- Contact manager if needed
