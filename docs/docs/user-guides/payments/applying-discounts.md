# Applying Discounts

This guide explains how to apply discounts to customer bills.

---

## When to Apply Discounts

**Apply discounts when:**
- Manager approves a discount
- Happy hour specials
- Customer loyalty discounts
- Promotional discounts
- Manager-approved exceptions

**Important:** 
- Servers may need manager approval
- Discounts are tracked for reporting
- Always enter a reason

---

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Stop the Table Session

**First, generate the bill:**

1. Click on occupied table
2. Click "Stop Session"
3. Review the bill total
4. Note the total amount

### Step 2: Open Payment Screen

**Click "Process Payment"**

**Payment screen appears**

### Step 3: Apply Discount

**Click "Apply Discount" button**

**Discount dialog appears:**
- Discount amount input
- Discount percentage input
- Reason field
- Preview of new total

### Step 4: Enter Discount

**Option 1: Dollar Amount**
- Enter discount amount (e.g., $10.00)
- System calculates new total

**Option 2: Percentage**
- Enter percentage (e.g., 15%)
- System calculates discount amount
- Shows dollar amount

**Example:**
- Bill: $100.00
- Discount: 10% = $10.00
- New total: $90.00

### Step 5: Enter Reason

**Enter reason for discount:**
- "Happy Hour"
- "Loyalty Customer"
- "Manager Approved"
- "Promotion"
- "Customer Complaint"

**Why this matters:**
- Tracks why discounts were given
- Helps with reporting
- Required for audit
- Manager can review

### Step 6: Get Approval (If Required)

**If you need manager approval:**

1. **Request approval** from manager
2. **Manager reviews** the request
3. **Manager approves or denies**
4. **If approved**, proceed with discount
5. **If denied**, process without discount

**Manager Approval:**
- May be required for discounts over certain amount
- May be required for all discounts
- Check with your manager on policy

### Step 7: Review Discounted Total

**System shows:**
- Original total: $100.00
- Discount: $10.00
- **New total: $90.00**

**Verify:**
- ✅ Discount amount is correct
- ✅ New total is correct
- ✅ Reason is entered

### Step 8: Apply and Process

**Click "Apply Discount"**

**What happens:**
- ✅ Discount is applied
- ✅ Total is updated
- ✅ Discount is recorded
- ✅ Ready to process payment

**Then process payment** with discounted amount

## Types of Discounts

### Fixed Amount Discount

**Example:**
- Bill: $75.00
- Discount: $10.00
- New total: $65.00

**When to use:**
- Specific dollar amount off
- Flat discount
- Simple calculations

### Percentage Discount

**Example:**
- Bill: $100.00
- Discount: 15%
- Discount amount: $15.00
- New total: $85.00

**When to use:**
- Percentage off
- Proportional discount
- Easy to calculate

### Happy Hour Discount

**Common scenario:**
- Specific time period (e.g., 4-6 PM)
- Percentage off drinks
- Automatic or manual application

**How to apply:**
- Check if it's happy hour time
- Apply discount to applicable items
- Or apply to entire bill
- Enter reason: "Happy Hour"

### Loyalty Customer Discount

**For repeat customers:**
- Customer has loyalty status
- Automatic or manual discount
- Percentage or fixed amount

**How to apply:**
- Verify customer is loyalty member
- Apply appropriate discount
- Enter reason: "Loyalty Customer"

### ⚠️ Discount Rules

- ✅ Get manager approval (if required)

- ✅ Enter reason for discount

## Manager Approval Process

### For Servers

**When you need approval:**

1. **Request from manager:**
   - Explain situation
   - State discount amount
   - Give reason

2. **Manager reviews:**
   - Checks if discount is appropriate
   - Verifies amount is reasonable
   - Approves or denies

3. **If approved:**
   - Apply discount in system
   - Enter manager's name in reason
   - Process payment

4. **If denied:**
   - Process payment without discount
   - Explain to customer
   - Follow manager's decision

### For Managers

**When approving discounts:**

1. **Review request:**
   - Is discount legitimate?
   - Is amount reasonable?
   - Does it follow policy?

2. **Make decision:**
   - Approve if appropriate
   - Deny if not appropriate
   - Adjust amount if needed

3. **Document:**
   - Enter approval in system
   - Note reason
   - Track for reporting

## Special Situations

### Discount on Specific Items Only

**If discount applies to certain items:**

- Apply discount to those items only
- Or calculate manually and enter total discount
- Note which items in reason field

### Multiple Discounts

**If multiple discounts apply:**

- Usually combine into one discount
- Or apply largest discount
- Check with manager on policy

### Discount Exceeds Bill Total

**If discount is more than bill:**

- Discount cannot exceed bill total
- Maximum discount = bill total
- System will prevent over-discounting

---

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Discount Not Allowed"

**Possible causes:**
- Manager approval required
- Discount amount too high
- System restriction

**Solutions:**
1. Get manager approval
2. Reduce discount amount
3. Check discount policy
4. Contact manager

### Problem: "Discount Amount Wrong"

**What to check:**
- Verify discount calculation
- Check percentage vs. amount
- Review discounted total
- Recalculate if needed

### Problem: "Can't Apply Discount"

**Possible causes:**
- System error
- Payment already processed
- Bill doesn't exist

**Solutions:**
1. Try again
2. Verify bill is active
3. Check payment hasn't been processed
4. Contact manager if issue persists
