# Updating Stock Levels (Managers)

This guide explains how managers update inventory stock levels.

---

## When to Update Stock

**Update stock when:**
- Delivery arrives from vendor
- Items are wasted/spoiled
- Manual inventory count
- Theft or loss occurs
- Stock adjustment needed

---

## Step-by-Step: Adding Stock

### When Delivery Arrives

1. **Click "Inventory"**
2. **Find the item** that was delivered
3. **Click "Adjust Stock"**
4. **Enter adjustment:**
   - **Method 1:** Enter new total quantity
   - **Method 2:** Enter adjustment amount (+50, -10, etc.)
5. **Select reason:**
   - "Delivery received"
   - "Manual count"
   - "Correction"
6. **Enter notes** (optional):
   - Delivery date
   - Vendor name
   - Invoice number
7. **Click "Save"**

**What happens:**
- Stock level updates immediately
- Adjustment is logged for audit
- Low stock alerts update
- Menu availability updates (if linked)

---

## Step-by-Step: Reducing Stock

### When Items are Wasted/Lost

1. **Click "Inventory"**
2. **Find the item**
3. **Click "Adjust Stock"**
4. **Enter negative adjustment:**
   - Example: -5 (reduce by 5)
   - Or enter new lower quantity
5. **Select reason:**
   - "Waste/Spillage"
   - "Theft/Loss"
   - "Expired"
   - "Damage"
6. **Enter notes** (optional)
7. **Click "Save"**

**What happens:**
- Stock level decreases
- Adjustment is logged
- System tracks the reduction
- Alerts update if needed

---

## Stock Adjustment Methods

### Method 1: Enter New Total

**Example:**
- Current stock: 25
- Delivery: +50
- New total: 75
- Enter: 75

**When to use:**
- After physical count
- When you know exact total
- For accuracy

### Method 2: Enter Adjustment Amount

**Example:**
- Current stock: 25
- Delivery: +50
- Adjustment: +50
- New total: 75 (calculated automatically)

**When to use:**
- When adding/subtracting
- For deliveries
- For waste/loss

---

## Important Notes

### ⚠️ Before Updating

- **Verify physical count** matches system
- **Check delivery invoice** matches items
- **Verify quantities** are correct
- **Double-check** before saving

### ✅ Best Practices

- Update stock promptly when deliveries arrive
- Enter accurate quantities
- Select appropriate reason
- Add notes for important adjustments
- Review adjustments regularly

### ❌ Common Mistakes

- Entering wrong quantities
- Forgetting to update after delivery
- Not selecting reason
- Skipping notes for important changes

### Common Reasons

**Adding Stock:**
- "Delivery received"

**Reducing Stock:**
- "Waste/Spillage"

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Stock Level Wrong"

**What to do:**
- Perform physical count
- Compare with system
- Adjust to match reality
- Investigate discrepancies

### Problem: "Can't Update Stock"

**Possible causes:**
- System error
- Permission issue
- Item doesn't exist

**Solutions:**
1. Try again
2. Check permissions
3. Verify item exists
4. Contact IT support
