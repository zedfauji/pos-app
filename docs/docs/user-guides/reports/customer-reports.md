# Customer Reports (Managers)

This guide explains how managers view customer reports and analytics.

---

## Overview

**Customer reports show:**
- Total customers
- Repeat customers
- Average customer value
- Customer segments
- Loyalty program status
- Customer lifetime value

---

## Viewing Customer Reports

### Step-by-Step

1. **Click "Reports"**
2. **Click "Customer Reports"**
3. **View:**
   - Total customers
   - Repeat customers percentage
   - Average customer value
   - Customer segments
   - Loyalty program members
   - Top customers

---

## Understanding Reports

### Total Customers

**Shows:**
- Number of unique customers
- New customers
- Total customer base

**Use for:**
- Understanding customer base
- Tracking growth
- Marketing planning

### Repeat Customers

**Shows:**
- Percentage of repeat customers
- Customer retention rate
- Loyalty metrics

**Use for:**
- Measuring customer loyalty
- Identifying retention issues
- Planning loyalty programs

### Average Customer Value

**Shows:**
- Average amount each customer spends
- Total revenue รท Number of customers
- Customer spending patterns

**Use for:**
- Understanding customer value
- Pricing decisions
- Marketing strategies

### Customer Segments

**Shows:**
- Customer groups (VIP, Regular, New)
- Spending by segment
- Segment sizes

**Use for:**
- Targeted marketing
- Personalized offers
- Customer management

### Loyalty Program

**Shows:**
- Number of loyalty members
- Points earned/redeemed
- Active members

**Use for:**
- Program effectiveness
- Engagement metrics
- Program improvements
