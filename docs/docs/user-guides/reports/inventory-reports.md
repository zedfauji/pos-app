# Inventory Reports (Managers)

This guide explains how managers view inventory reports and analytics.

---

## Overview

**Inventory reports show:**
- Current stock levels
- Low stock items
- Items sold (quantities)
- Inventory value
- Turnover rates
- Reorder recommendations

---

## Viewing Inventory Reports

### Step-by-Step

1. **Click "Reports"**
2. **Click "Inventory Reports"**
3. **View:**
   - Current stock levels
   - Low stock alerts
   - Items sold today/week/month
   - Inventory value
   - Turnover rates
   - Reorder recommendations

---

## Understanding Reports

### Current Stock Levels

**Shows:**
- All items in inventory
- Current quantities
- Status (In Stock, Low Stock, Out of Stock)
- Reorder thresholds

**Use for:**
- Quick stock check
- Identifying low stock items
- Planning orders

### Low Stock Items

**Shows:**
- Items below reorder threshold
- How much below threshold
- Recommended order quantity

**Use for:**
- Planning reorders
- Preventing stockouts
- Managing inventory

### Items Sold

**Shows:**
- Quantities sold per item
- Time period (today, week, month)
- Sales trends

**Use for:**
- Understanding demand
- Planning inventory
- Identifying popular items

### Inventory Value

**Shows:**
- Total value of inventory
- Value per item
- Cost analysis

**Use for:**
- Financial planning
- Cost management
- Budgeting

### Turnover Rates

**Shows:**
- How fast items sell
- Days of stock remaining
- Sales velocity

**Use for:**
- Ordering decisions
- Inventory optimization
- Demand forecasting

---

## Exporting Reports

### Step-by-Step

1. **View inventory report**
2. **Click "Export"**
3. **Select format:**
   - PDF
   - Excel
   - CSV
4. **Download**

---

## Quick Reference

### To View Reports:
```
1. Click "Reports"
2. Click "Inventory Reports"
3. View desired information
4. Export if needed
```
